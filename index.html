Information about data

  • Information is collected from crafts in game and witnessed by eye. Therefore data is approximate and is listed only as a reference for collecting your own science in game and planning rescources for missions
  • Times listed are based on Kerbin time. 6 hours is equal to 1 Kerbin day
  • Example: 20 hours is equal to 3 kerbin days and 2 hours
  • Shadow times do not account for eclispes of other bodies or shadow from mountains or crater walls.
